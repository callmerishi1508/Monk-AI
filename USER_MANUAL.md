# NEXUS AI: Official User Manual

Welcome to NEXUS AI. This manual is designed to guide operators—from Executives to Analysts—on how to effectively navigate the platform, interact with the Intelligence Copilot, and utilize the Temporal Graph to conduct deep strategic analysis.

---

## 1. Getting Started: The Command Interface

When you log into NEXUS, you are presented with a unified command dashboard. 

### 1.1 Selecting Your Persona
NEXUS adapts its interface and the intelligence it provides based on your role.
*   **Locate the Persona Selector:** Found in the top navigation bar.
*   **Action:** Select your role (e.g., `Chief Executive Officer`, `Threat Intelligence`, or `Analyst`).
*   **Result:** The interface will instantly morph. Executive roles will see a clean, high-level strategic overview, while Analyst roles will see deeper, granular data feeds and raw event logs.

### 1.2 Filtering by Sector
NEXUS tracks multiple industries simultaneously. To avoid information overload, you must narrow your focus.
*   **Locate the Sector Dropdown:** Found next to the Persona Selector.
*   **Action:** Choose a sector such as `Cyber Security`, `Fintech`, or `Productivity SaaS`.
*   **Result:** The Knowledge Graph and the Copilot will instantly restrict their knowledge to that specific industry vertical.

---

## 2. Exploring the Knowledge Graph

The centerpiece of NEXUS is the Temporal Knowledge Graph. It visually represents the state of the market, showing competitors and the "Signals" (events/moves) connected to them.

*   **Navigation:** Click and drag to pan around the graph. Scroll up or down to zoom in and out.
*   **Reading the Map:** 
    *   **Purple Nodes:** Represent `Company` entities (e.g., CrowdStrike, Microsoft).
    *   **Teal/Orange Nodes:** Represent `Signals` (e.g., Market Expansion, Pricing Compression).
*   **De-Cluttered View:** To keep the screen clean, Signal names are hidden by default. 
    *   **Action:** **Hover** your mouse over any small Signal node to reveal its label.
*   **Deep Dive:** 
    *   **Action:** **Click** on any node.
    *   **Result:** The Copilot and the Explainability Panel on the right will immediately focus on that specific entity or event, providing you with detailed context.

---

## 3. The Strategic Intelligence Copilot

The Copilot is your AI assistant, but unlike standard chatbots, it is strictly governed by the evidence visible in your active Knowledge Graph.

*   **Location:** The chat terminal on the right side of your screen.
*   **Action:** Type a strategic question. For example: *"What is the primary threat in the Fintech sector right now?"* or *"Summarize recent regulatory filings."*
*   **Result:** The Copilot will answer the question in the tone of your selected Persona (e.g., a CEO will receive a high-level strategic brief; an Analyst will receive tactical details).

### 3.1 The Explainability Panel (Zero-Trust Verification)
NEXUS operates on "Zero-Trust AI." Whenever the Copilot makes a claim, you can verify it.
*   **Action:** Look at the **Explainability Panel** below or next to the Copilot.
*   **Result:** You will see the exact raw text snippet (e.g., a scanned SEC filing or a competitor's press release) that the AI used to generate its answer, alongside a mathematical confidence score.

---

## 4. Time Travel (The Temporal Rail)

NEXUS does not just track the present; it remembers the past perfectly. You can rewind the state of the market to see what the Graph looked like before major events occurred.

*   **Location:** The Timeline Slider (Temporal Rail) at the bottom or side of your screen.
*   **Action 1 (Enable Replay):** Toggle the system from `LIVE` mode to `REPLAY` mode.
*   **Action 2 (Scrub Time):** Click and drag the slider back to a historical milestone (e.g., "January 2024").
*   **Result:** Watch the Knowledge Graph physically rewind. Nodes that did not exist at that time will disappear, and the Copilot will "forget" anything that happened after that date.

---

## 5. Monitoring System Integrity

To ensure you are acting on live, stable data, you should monitor the system's health.

*   **Location:** The Integrity Dashboard (usually the left panel).
*   **Database Sync:** Ensure this reads `HEALTHY`. This means NEXUS is actively communicating with its core database.
*   **Governance State:** If this turns red and reads `GOVERNANCE_FROZEN`, it means the system has detected an overwhelming amount of contradictory information and has paused new data ingestion to protect your strategic intelligence from corruption.

---

### Need Further Assistance?
If you encounter visual anomalies, ensure your browser zoom is set to 100%. If the Copilot stops responding, check the System Integrity panel to ensure your connection to the NEXUS core is active.
